What is Mud Mavericks?

Mud Mavericks is a fast-paced motocross board game where players race across muddy tracks packed with jumps, hazards, tight corners, and boost zones.

Every turn is a choice: push your bike for speed or play it safe to avoid a crash. Smart risks and perfect timing decide who wins.

Why Players Love It

  • 🔥 High-speed motocross racing
  • ⚡ Risk vs reward decisions every turn
  • 💥 Crashes, damage, and big comeback moments
  • 🎯 Easy to learn, hard to master
  • 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Fun for kids, teens, and adults

Game Details

Players: 2–5

Ages: 7+

Play Time: 30–60 minutes

Genre: Racing • Strategy • Push-Your-Luck

Built for Replayability

Every race feels different thanks to changing track layouts (with the expansion pack), unpredictable card effects, multiple riding strategies, and even 5 different bikes.

Whether you're leading the pack or chasing a risky comeback, Mud Mavericks keeps everyone engaged until the finish line.
