What is Mud Mavericks?

Mud Mavericks is a fast-paced motocross board game where players race across muddy tracks packed with jumps, hazards, tight corners, and boost zones.

Every turn is a choice: push your bike for speed or play it safe to avoid a crash. Smart risks and perfect timing decide who wins.
