What is Mud Mavericks?

Mud Mavericks is a high-energy motocross board game where players race across muddy tracks filled with jumps, hazards, tight corners, and boost zones.

Every turn is a decision: push your bike for speed or play it safe to avoid crashes. Skill, timing, and guts decide who wins.
